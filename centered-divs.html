How to center a div with CSS?

  • Text align: center
  • margin: 0 auto;
  • Absolute position, negative margin
  • Absolute position, negative transform: translate()
  • Flexbox
text-align: center;
width: 100px; margin: 0 auto;
Position absolute: top and left 50% and negative top and left margins of the half of the centered element
Position absolute: top and left 50% and transform: translate(-50%, -50%);
Flex item centered
